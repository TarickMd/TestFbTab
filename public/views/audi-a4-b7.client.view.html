The Audi A4 (B7) first went on sale in 2005 with production coming to an end in 2008. The convertible B7 was produced from 2007 to 2008.

The class of the Audi A4 (B7), also known as the Typ 8E/8H, can be described as an impressive compact executive class of car manufactured by German car producer Audi. The B7 car was the third model launched from the A4 Audi range. This model was produced in three different body styles including a 4 door saloon/sedan, 2 door cabriolet and a 5 door Avant estate/wagon. This stylish compact executive car had additional features including navigation systems and electronic stability programme. The vehicle came with different petrol and diesel engine options ranging from 1.6 litre to 3.2 litre.

The measurements of the Audi A4 (B7) are as follows:

  • Length: four door saloon 4,586mm / cabriolet 4,573mm
  • Width: four door saloon 1,772mm / cabriolet 1,777mm
  • Height: four door saloon 1,427mm / cabriolet 1,518mm
  • Wheelbase: four door saloon 2,648mm
